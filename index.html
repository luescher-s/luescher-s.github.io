Dr. Sandro Lüscher

Welcome to my personal webpage. I’m Sandro Lüscher, a political scientist and postdoctoral researcher at the Centre for Democracy Studies Aarau (ZDA), University of Zurich. My work lies at the intersection of political science and computational approaches, exploring how digital technologies and data-driven methods can help us understand and improve democratic processes and institutions.

Digital democracy, democratic innovations, Swiss politics, electoral systems and reforms, political representation.
