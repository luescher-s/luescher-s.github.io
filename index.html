About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage! I’m Sandro Lüscher, a political scientist and a postdoctoral researcher at the Centre for Democracy Studies in Aarau (since January 2025) after earning my PhD at the Department of Political Science, University of Zurich in November 2024. I am currently working on how new digital technologies could be integrated into democratic governance.
Fields of interest: Digital democracy, democratic innovations, Swiss politics, electoral politics, electoral systems (and their reforms), political representation.
