About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage. I’m Sandro Lüscher, a political scientist and postdoctoral researcher at the Centre for Democracy Studies in Aarau after earning my PhD at the Department of Political Science, University of Zurich. I am currently working on how new digital technologies could be integrated into democratic governance.

Fields of interest: Digital democracy, democratic innovations, Swiss politics, electoral politics, electoral systems (and their reforms), political representation.
